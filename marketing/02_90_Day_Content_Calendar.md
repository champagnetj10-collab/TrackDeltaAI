# TrackDelta AI — 90-Day Social Content Calendar

Covers YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, X, LinkedIn, Facebook. Read [01_Brand_Voice_and_Style_Guide.md](01_Brand_Voice_and_Style_Guide.md) first.

**How this calendar is built:** rather than 90 mechanically pre-written daily posts (which go stale the moment a real session, a bug fix, or a driver comment gives you something better to post), this defines the *system* — content pillars, weekly themes, and platform cadence — plus fully worked examples for three representative weeks so execution is unambiguous. Slot real content into this structure week by week.

---

## 1. Content Pillars (every post is one of these)

| Pillar | % of output | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Education** | 30% | Braking/throttle/racing line/racecraft content (file 09). Builds trust and SEO/discovery independent of the product. |
| **Product Proof** | 25% | Real (or clearly-labeled illustrative) Driver DNA reports, debriefs, before/after lap times. Answers "why is this not just MoTeC." |
| **Building in Public** | 20% | Development progress, decisions, honest setbacks (file 08). |
| **Community / Creator** | 15% | Reactions to, collabs with, or shoutouts of sim racing creators and driver-submitted results. |
| **Culture / Relatable** | 10% | Lighter, funny, "every sim racer knows this feeling" content. Keeps the account human between the denser posts. |

## 2. Platform Cadence

| Platform | Frequency | Format | Primary pillar lean |
|---|---|---|---|
| YouTube Shorts | 5–7/week | 15–45s vertical | Education, Product Proof |
| TikTok | 5–7/week | 15–45s vertical | Culture, Education |
| Instagram Reels | 4–5/week | 15–45s vertical (repurpose Shorts/TikTok) | Product Proof, Culture |
| X (Twitter) | 1–3/day | Text, quote-card image, or native video | Building in Public, Product Proof |
| LinkedIn | 2–3/week | Text post, founder voice | Building in Public |
| Facebook | 3–4/week | Repurposed Reels + link posts into sim racing groups (where permitted) | Education, Community |
| YouTube long-form | 1/week | 6–15 min | Education, Product Proof, Building in Public (rotating) |

Short-form video ideas referenced below (SF-01 … SF-50) come from [03_Short_Form_Video_Ideas.md](03_Short_Form_Video_Ideas.md). Long-form (LF-01 … LF-20) come from [04_Long_Form_YouTube_Outlines.md](04_Long_Form_YouTube_Outlines.md).

## 7. Monthly Reporting Checkpoint

At the end of each 30-day block (day 30, 60, 90), review against the North Star metric context from the PRD — are viewers converting to sign-ups, are sign-ups converting to a second session? Content that drives *sessions*, not just views, gets doubled down on. Use this to decide which of LF-14–LF-20 (the evergreen bank) to release and which short-form pillar needs more weight in the next 30 days.

---

## 8. Non-Negotiables (apply every single day)

- Never post a claim the product can't back up (see brand voice guide §1)
- Every video's on-screen graphics match the actual app's visual language
- Watermark/logo bug in the corner of every video
- Caption always includes one clear CTA (see file 03 for the CTA bank)
- If a post underperforms, don't delete it — data compounds; deleting also erases learning
